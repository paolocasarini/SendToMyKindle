FAQ

How can I remove my account from this service?

Just write to sendtomykindle@casarini.org and ask for it.

Which kind of documents will be converted and delivered?

It depends on Amazon rules but at the moment:

  • Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx)
  • Rich Text Format (.rtf)
  • HTML (.htm, .html)
  • Text (.txt) documents
  • Archived documents (zip , x-zip) and compressed archived documents
  • Mobi book

Images that are of type JPEGs (.jpg), GIFs (.gif), Bitmaps (.bmp), and PNG images (.png).

Adobe PDF (.pdf) documents are delivered without conversion to Kindle DX, Second Generation and Latest Generation Kindles.
Conversion of Adobe PDF (.pdf) to Kindle format is done on experimental basis.

Where are the files I have put the app folder?

The files delivered to your kindle are deleted from the app folder.

Don't use the app folder to archive your documents, just copy them there only when you want to send them to your kindle account.
