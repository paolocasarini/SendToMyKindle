Overview

SendToMyKindle is a DropBox App to send files from DropBox to your Kindle account through the @kindle.com @free.kindle.com email address.

How to use it

  1. Visit your Kindle's setting page and add sendtomykindle@casarini.org to the approved email list.
  2. Enter your Kindle's email address here:
  3. Allow the app on Dropbox within your account clicking the button below:

Once you have allowed the app to use your Dropbox account, your app folder (/Apps/SentToMyKindle) will be scanned regularly without futher actions needed from you.

Just put the files that you want to send to your kindle account the app folder and after few minutes you'll find them on your kindle.

Enjoy!
